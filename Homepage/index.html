ABOUT OUR PRODUCT

Speechless is a video calling platform that translates sign language in real time in the form of subtitles during a call. Currently, it supports the reading of the 26 letters of the English alphabet as well as a "space" character for separating words.

OUR INSPIRATION

Accessibility is important.
Today, around one million people use American Sign Language as their main way to communicate, yet the majority of the world cannot use or understand it. Thus, we aimed to solve this communication problem by building a video chatting platform where users are able to call their loved ones and chat using sign language. The platform translates words in real time, displaying them as live subtitles in the video screen during the call so that users are able to easily communicate despite the restrictions of their accessibility needs.

HOW TO USE

1. First create an access code to the chat room.

2. Enter click start to go to the chat room.

3. Share your code with your friends and start the video chat.

4. Start chatting!
